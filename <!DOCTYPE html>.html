About Waco Aero

Welcome to Waco Aero, a student-led aerospace research and development club dedicated to hands-on learning in aviation, rocketry, and aerospace engineering. Our members design, build, and test aircraft, conduct materials research, and explore the fascinating world of flight.

Founded in 2025, our club brings together students from multiple schools across our city who are passionate about aerospace to collaborate on real-world engineering projects. We believe in learning by doing, rigorous testing, and thorough documentation of our findings.
