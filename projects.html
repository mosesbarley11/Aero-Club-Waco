Summer Fixed-Wing Builds & First Flights

Summer 2025 Fixed-Wing Completed

Our first real club flights — assembling and flying the FlightTest FT Explorer foam board trainer, alongside a set of 3D-printed aircraft. These sessions gave us hands-on experience with aircraft assembly, CG balancing, control surface setup, and hand-launch techniques before ever turning on the transmitter.
